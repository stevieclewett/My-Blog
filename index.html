About me

Hi my name is Stevie. I grew up in the Katy, TX bubble. I graduated from Seven Lakes High School in a class of almost 1,000 students. I have three siblings Shayne (26), Sammie (20), and Beau (16). My favorite little girl in the world (my niece), was born in December of 2020, so I guess 2020 wasn’t so bad after all! My parents, Shannon and Sabryna, are the hardest working, coolest parents I could have asked for. They instilled in all four of us from a young age that we are not owed anything in life, anything we want out of life, we have to work for.

In high school, I was Vice President of my class, President of my National Charity League chapter, and played Varsity golf. In college, I played on the women’s golf team, Vice President of the Dance Marathon Committee, and President of the Student Athlete Advisory Committee. What can I say, I like to be involved and in charge?

When I’m not doing homework or working, I enjoy reading, golfing, hanging out with friends, and DIY projects. In college, I realized that I was more creative than I gave myself credit for... I realized I actually do enjoy art, interior design, and painting. Unfortunately, by the end of my college career, I also learned accounting is not really my speed (read my “Why Accounting!?” blog post to learn more about this).

I started this website so I could let you know more about me than a resume could. Although I am proud of what my resume does have to show about me, I am more proud of the things and people that this website and my blogs will reveal about me.
