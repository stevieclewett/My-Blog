About me

Hi my name is Stevie. I grew up in Katy, TX where I graduated from Seven Lakes High School. I have three siblings Shayne (26), Sammie (20), and Beau (16). My niece, my favorite little girl in the world, was born in December of 2020 - so I guess 2020 wasn’t so bad after all! My parents, Shannon and Sabryna, are the hardest working and coolest parents I could have asked for. They instilled in all four of us from a young age that we are not owed anything, if we want something out of life, we have to work for it and go get it ourselves.

In high school, I was the Vice President of my class, President of my National Charity League chapter, and played Varsity golf. In college, I played on the Women’s Golf Team, Vice President of the Dance Marathon Committee, and President of the Student Athlete Advisory Committee.

What can I say, I like to be involved and meet new people!

When I’m not doing homework or working, I enjoy reading, golfing, and DIY projects. In college, I realized that I was more creative than I gave myself credit for... I realized I actually do enjoy art, interior design, and painting. Right now, I am trying to figure out what my next few steps in life are and finding a fulfilling career that I can excel and grow in.

I started this website to help give people a better understanding of what makes me me. Although I am proud of all the acomplishments, skills, and experiences on my resume I don't believe it gives the full picture.
